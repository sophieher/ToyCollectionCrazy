    Scarygirl Edition

    The App for Collectors

    Toy Collection Crazy! Scarygirl Edition is an app that will allow you to showcase your Nathan Jurevicius Scarygirl toy collection!

      Check out all of the Scarygirl toys.
      Add toys to your "My Toys" collection.
      Create custom toys.
      Sign up for updates and alerts.

    Toy Screens

    Clicking on Toys! will take you to the toy list right away where you will find all of the Scarygirl toys and their set names.
    You will see there are three tabs: All Toys, My Toys, and Collectors (coming soon...).
    Pick a toy to see it's year and picture!


      Have a toy not featured in the list. That's easy, you can add your own custom toys!

    Add Custom Toys

    Just click on the plus icon from the toy list pages and you can add your own custom toys.
    Take a picture and fill out the form. Check the "I Own This" box.
    And you will see the new toy in your "My Toy"s list to share with your friends.


      Made a mistake? No problem. You can delete your custom toys with ease.

    Get Notified

    Sign in with Google authentication. Just use the accounts you have already on your device.
    Then, Register for notification alerts.
